Our Services

Services

Tigergen Manpower and Allied Services Inc. is an authorized recruitment agency accredited to recruit qualified individuals and a trusted provider of the labor force in the Philippines since 2013. Our services include: maintenance services, manpower services, construction workers, tuna processing workers, cold storage workers, call center agents, and more.

Branches and Clients

Tigergen Manpower and Allied Services have been handling and accomodating numerous clients in Mindanao not only in General Santos City but also in other places such as: Maasim, Sarangani Province; Kidapawan, North Cotabato; and Toril, Davao City.
We also seek to expand our services and provide assistance to the organizations searching for qualified labor force throughout the nation.
